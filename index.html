LEAGUE OF LEGENDS

League of Legends, released in 2009 by Riot Games, is a global multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game where players control powerful champions with unique abilities in team-based battles to destroy the enemy Nexus. Beyond the competitive gameplay, it offers a sprawling universe called Runeterra, featuring over 160 champions, diverse regions, and interconnected lore that explores conflict, ambition, and identity. Spin-offs like Teamfight Tactics and Legends of Runeterra offer alternative strategic experiences, while the animated series Arcane (2021) provides narrative depth, exploring personal stories, class struggle, and the consequences of ambition. Together, these games and media expand the League universe, delivering a blend of intense strategy, emergent storytelling, and emotional engagement that continues to evolve within esports and global entertainment.
